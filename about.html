How We Got Here

A Move Across the Country.
A Life Rooted in Kentucky.

Life Built in Kentucky was born out of lived experience, not a marketing plan. After packing up our family and moving across the country with more questions than answers, we found ourselves drawn to Kentucky for its land, its people, and the space it offers to build a life with intention.

Our lived experiences with renovating homes and searching for our own home quickly became a calling. We walked raw land, navigated renovations, lived through builds, and learned sometimes the hard way what it actually takes to turn property into a place that supports real life.

"Most people don't just need a Realtor. They need a guide who understands the land, the build process, the finances, and the long-term vision."

Along the way, we realized that truth firsthand. And it changed everything about how we approach this work.

The Person Behind It

Meet Toni Schafer,
Realtor & General Contractor

Toni Schafer is a licensed Kentucky Realtor and one of the few female General Contractors in Central Kentucky. That combination isn't just a credential it's a completely different way of seeing a property, a deal, and a client's future.

When Toni walks through a home with a buyer, she's seeing the bones. The real renovation costs. The land's potential. The things that take years of build experience to spot. That knowledge protects her clients and opens doors that most agents simply can't see.

Whether you're a first-time buyer, a family ready to build from scratch, or an investor looking at land Toni brings the same thing every time: honesty, deep local knowledge, and the kind of commitment that comes from doing work you genuinely believe in.
